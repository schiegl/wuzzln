Rules

The game rules are largely based on the rules from the International Table Soccer Federation (ITSF). However, we added a few rules to spice things up. Below you will find the most important ones:

  1. If the ball passes the goal line it counts as a point (even if it bounces back out)
  2. If a team scores a goal, their opponent gets the ball at their 5er row
  3. If the ball falls off the table
    • Falls off in the middle: The ball is thrown in the middle
    • Otherwise: The ball is placed in the closest corner
  4. The ball must be passed twice within the 5er row before a round starts
  5. If a team commits a foul, the oppenent gets the ball at their 5er row. The most important fouls are:
    • Scoring a goal before passing the ball twice within the starting team
    • Rotating a player more than 360¬∞ shortly before or after touching the ball
    • Strong impacts to the table, such that the opponent is distracted or the ball is deflected (e.g. moving the table)
  6. If the ball comes to a halt
    • Between the 5er rows: The team who lost the last point gets it (or it is rolled in again if no goals were scored yet)
    • Otherwise: The nearest defense gets the ball

  8. If a lower ranking player challenges higher ranking player
    • The higher ranking player declines: The lower ranking player wins 5:1
    • The higher ranking player has legitimately no time: Nothing happens
    • The lower ranking challenged the higher ranking player already this: Wait for the next day, unless it's last day of the season
    • The lower ranking player can also pick the side
